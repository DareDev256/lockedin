Interactive
Write Your NFC Tag
Paste your LockedIn URL and tap the button while holding your phone to an NFC tag.
Browser support: Web NFC works on Chrome for Android only. iOS users — use the free NFC Tools app (instructions below).

Using the NFC Tools app (iOS + Android)

  1. Download NFC Tools — free on App Store or Google Play
  2. Open the app and tap Write
  3. Tap Add a record → choose URL / URI
  4. Paste your LockedIn URL into the field
  5. Tap Write and hold your phone to the NFC tag
  6. Done — anyone who taps the tag will see your card
